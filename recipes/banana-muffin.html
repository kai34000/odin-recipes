Banana Muffin

Description

This banana muffin recipe is vegan! Banana is usually a great substitute for eggs in baking so this recipe is totally perfect! This recipe is great for people who don't like sweets too sweet, if you get what I mean. It is moist, nutty, and sweet but not too sweet.Is your mouth watering yet? So let's get baking!

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. 1/2 tsp baking soda
  3. 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. 1/4 tps salt
  5. 3/4 tps cinnamon powder
  6. 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  7. 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  8. 1/2 cup oat milk
  9. 3 tbps vegan butter: softened but not melted
  10. 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  11. 1/2 tps vanilla extract
  12. 2 large ripe bananas
  13. 1/2 cup chopped walnut chunks

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees farenheight.
  2. Combine all the dry ingredients in one bowl;the flour,baking soda,baking powder, salt,cinnamon powder,granulated sugar,light brown sugar. Stir untill well combined.
  3. Pour all wet ingredients into the bowl from step 2;the butter, oil, and milk. Fold everything in with a spatula, just until combined, do not overmix.
  4. In another bowl, mash peeled bananas until you achieve smooth consistensy with no chunks.
  5. Pour the mashed banana into the mix from step 3 and fold everything until combined, but do not overmix.
  6. Add in the walnut chunks to the mix and fold lightly.
  7. Transfer the mixture into a muffin pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
